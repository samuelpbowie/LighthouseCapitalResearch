Investment Thesis

Okta is the independent identity platform of record for the enterprise. In a world where the perimeter has dissolved and identity has become the new security boundary, Okta sits at a structurally advantaged position — deeply embedded in customer IT stacks with high switching costs, expanding wallet share through Customer Identity Cloud (Auth0), and benefiting from secular tailwinds in zero-trust security adoption.

After a turbulent period marked by the Auth0 integration and the 2023 security incident, Okta has stabilized its go-to-market and is executing on a path toward durable free cash flow generation. We believe the market continues to overly discount near-term growth deceleration while underappreciating the long-term platform stickiness and Rule of 40 trajectory.

Bull vs. Bear

Bull Case

  • Best-of-breed independent identity — not tied to a hyperscaler
  • Customer Identity (Auth0) represents underpenetrated second vector
  • Zero-trust mandates accelerate enterprise IAM spending
  • Durable net revenue retention as customers expand seats and modules
  • Significant operating leverage as growth stabilizes

Bear Case

  • Microsoft Entra bundling pressure in Windows-heavy enterprises
  • Brand damage from 2023 security incident lingers in sales cycles
  • Growth deceleration compresses multiple further
  • Intensifying competition from CyberArk, Ping, and hyperscalers
  • Path to GAAP profitability remains multi-year
